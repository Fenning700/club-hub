Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

ClubHub ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform, including our WhatsApp, Gmail, and AI integrations.

1. Who We Are

ClubHub is a grassroots club secretary management platform designed to help clubs automate administrative tasks through integrations with WhatsApp, Gmail, and AI-powered tools.

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at: hello@clubhub.com

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Message content: WhatsApp and Gmail messages processed through our platform, used solely to provide our service
  • Usage data: How you interact with our platform (pages visited, features used)
  • Club information: Details about your club that you provide to us

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only as necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • To operate the ClubHub platform and its integrations
  • To process and respond to WhatsApp and Gmail messages on your behalf
  • To provide AI-powered automation of administrative tasks
  • To communicate with you about your account or our service
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. WhatsApp Messaging

When you use our WhatsApp integration, we process message content solely to facilitate communication between you and your club members. Specifically:

  • Message content is used only to support the relevant conversation
  • We do not share the content of one customer's chat with any other customer
  • We do not request or store sensitive identifiers such as payment card numbers or government ID numbers via WhatsApp
  • You can opt out of WhatsApp communications at any time by contacting us or replying STOP

5. Gmail Integration

If you connect a Gmail account, we access only the emails necessary to perform the tasks you have authorised. We do not read, store, or share email content beyond what is required to deliver the service.

6. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Third-party tools that help us operate our platform (e.g. hosting, AI providers), who are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Legal authorities: Where required by law

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties without your explicit consent.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@clubhub.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary for the platform to function. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date.
